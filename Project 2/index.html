About me

I love the smiths.

Hi, I'm Michelle

Hi!, I'm Michelle. You can call me mich! I am an 19-year-old female with a birthdate on October 16, 2004. I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information System at Kolehiyo Lungsod ng Dasmarinas. As for my nationality, I am proudly Filipino. In terms of my educational background, I completed my secondary education at Dasmarinas Integrated High School and graduated from Sampaloc Elementary School for my elementary education. Currently, I am continuing my academic journey at the tertiary level in Kolehiyo Lungsod ng Dasmarinas.

I'm a self taught artist.

Ever since childhood, my passion for art has remained unwavering. Starting with anime drawings, my curiosity led me to explore diverse art forms, including watercolor, acrylics, oils, and digital art. Venturing into the realm of realism became an integral part of my artistic journey, and reflecting on this, I can confidently say that I've achieved an intermediate level as an artist. If you're interested in discussing commissions, don't hesitate to reach out!

Chungking Express | 1994

Chungking Express, directed by Wong Kar-wai, is a visually captivating and emotionally resonant Hong Kong film. This is a masterpiece of mine that I am really proud of. I painted this on June 15, 2022. What inspired me in this is the visual aesthetics of the character and the emotions behind it. It took me almost a week to finish this painting and I love the process in doing it. I painted this using Art Rangers acrylics and Winson and Newton oil paint on my canvas.

Flowerboy

This is my second favorite painting that I did. It was inspired by Tyler the Creator's album "Flowerboy" and was painted using the Art Ranger and Sakura acrylic paint. This painting of mine often brings back memories of my music tasteÂ in high school.
